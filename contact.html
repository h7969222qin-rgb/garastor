Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request flooring samples?

Requesting samples is easy and free. Select "Sample Request" as your inquiry type in the contact form above, let us know which collections or colors you're interested in, and we'll deliver samples to your door within 3–5 business days. You can also visit our factory to browse the full material library in person.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation through our nationwide network of certified flooring installers. Each team is trained in GARASTOR-specific techniques to ensure a flawless finish. Select "Installation Support" in your inquiry and we'll connect you with an approved installation partner in your area.

Can I order custom flooring dimensions?

Absolutely. Our custom milling service can accommodate virtually any size or pattern requirement — from extra-wide planks to custom parquet patterns. Contact us with your specifications and our design team will work with you to create the exact flooring solution you envision. Lead times vary based on complexity and volume.

How do I maintain and care for my floor?

Proper care ensures your GARASTOR floor lasts for decades. We recommend using our specially formulated cleaning products, avoiding excessive water and harsh chemicals, and placing protective pads under furniture. Our floors come with a comprehensive warranty, and we offer professional refinishing services. Contact us with any maintenance questions — we're happy to guide you.
